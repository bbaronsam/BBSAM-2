Hello, I am Baron Sam, an accomplished educator with a diverse academic background and nearly 13 years of experience in higher education. My journey has led me through esteemed institutions such as Ponjesly College of Engineering, Sathyabama University, and VelTech Multitech Dr.RR & Dr.SR Engineering College. With a Ph.D. in Medical Image Processing, an MBA in Human Resource Management, and extensive expertise in computer science and engineering. I am passionate about imparting knowledge and nurturing the next generation of professionals. I am committed to empowering students to excel both academically and professionally. I am excited about the opportunity to further engage with students, colleagues, and industry partners in a dynamic and challenging environment, where I can continue to evolve as an educator and make a lasting impact on the field of higher education.
