Dr. B. Baron Sam

About Me

Dr. Baron Sam was born in Kanyakumari, India, in 1988. He received his Bachelors degree in B.Tech Information Technology from Anna University Chennai. He completed his B.Ed from Tamil Nadu Teachers Education University. His Masters Degree in M.E Computer Science from Veltech Technical university, Chennai, Master of Business Administration in Human Resource Management from Annamalai University, Chidambaram and Ph.D degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Sathyabama University, Chennai, India. Currently he is working as an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Ponjesly College of Engineering, Nagercoil, India. His-current research interests include Image Processing, Internet of things and Artificial Intelligence.

Area Of Interests : Internet of Things, Image Processing, Computer Networks
Programming Languages : Python, C, Java, C++








From Dr. Baron Sam

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Dr. B Baron Sam, an Associate Professor and Head of Department of AI & DS at DMI Engineering College, with a passion for education and a dedication to nurturing the next generation of professionals. With over 13 years of experience in higher education, I've cultivated a diverse skill set that includes expertise in programming, debugging, and lecturing. Armed with a PhD, I'm passionate about leveraging my skills to drive impactful solutions and deliver tangible results. Outside of work, I find joy in engaging with electrical works, repairing bikes, and tinkering with electronics. These hands-on activities provide me with a creative outlet and a sense of accomplishment outside of the classroom. Looking ahead, my aspiration is to become a Principal, where I can leverage my leadership skills and passion for education to drive positive change and foster a culture of excellence. I am driven by the opportunity to make a lasting impact on the educational landscape. In my quest for personal and professional growth, I am eager to engage in a dynamic and challenging environment where I can continue to evolve as an educator and contribute meaningfully to the field of higher education.
